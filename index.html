Mystery Mixture

Students combine unknown substances and use evidence (gas production, temperature change, color change) to determine if a chemical reaction occurred. Works in baggies OR an ice cube tray for wells.

About

Made by a teacher who knows the struggle.

In A Pinch was born out of a real problem: science teachers are expected to run hands-on labs with shrinking budgets, no prep time, and limited access to materials. I've been that teacher.

With 7 years in the classroom and a background as a medical laboratory scientist, I won a national grand prize from the Shell Science Lab Challenge and built the tool I always needed.
